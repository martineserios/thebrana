## PARA Structure

Four categories organized by **actionability**, not topic:

| Category | Definition | Time horizon | Example |
|----------|-----------|--------------|---------|
| **Projects** | Tasks connected to a goal with a deadline | Days to months | "Rewrite landing page by Q2" |
| **Areas** | Ongoing responsibilities with a standard to maintain | Indefinite | Health, finances, family |
| **Resources** | Topics/interests with no responsibility to maintain | Optional | Business books, travel tips |
| **Archives** | Completed projects, retired areas | Historical | Past client work |

**Key principle:** Organize by actionability, not by topic. Current commitments first.

## CODE Method

| Step | What | Key |
|------|------|-----|
| **Capture** | Selectively save ideas worth keeping | Be selective — not everything |
| **Organize** | Place by current project relevance | "How can this help something I'm working on?" |
| **Distill** | Transform into your own language | Extract key insights |
| **Express** | Use distilled notes as building blocks | Turn consumption into creation |

## Progressive Summarization

Layered distillation:
1. **Layer 1:** Full original text
2. **Layer 2:** Bold the best parts
3. **Layer 3:** Highlight the "best of the best"
4. **Layer 4:** Executive summary in your own words (top notes only)

Double utility — reviewing for current use simultaneously summarizes for future discovery.

## Personal vs Professional

PARA does NOT prescriptively separate them. Create Areas for each (Work, Health, Family) within one unified system. No structural fragmentation.

## Strengths

- Beginner-friendly, minimal learning curve
- System-agnostic (works in any tool)
- Actionable focus — organizes around current reality
- Flexible, scales with volume

## Weaknesses (Critical)

- **"Beautiful organized graveyard"** — capture, file perfectly, never revisit. No built-in rediscovery.
- **Search degrades at scale** — no guaranteed serendipitous connections
- **Not a thinking tool** — organizes execution, not cognition. No idea interconnection (unlike Zettelkasten)
- **Execution bias** — assumes current projects are most valuable. Old insights that become relevant later get lost.
- **No knowledge compounding** — fails at long-term pattern recognition

## vs Miessler PAI

PAI is infrastructure for AI collaboration; PARA is pure knowledge management. PAI assumes you work with an AI assistant. PARA predates this pattern.

## vs Johnny Decimal

| Dimension | PARA | Johnny Decimal |
|-----------|------|----------------|
| Philosophy | "Where does this belong NOW?" | "Where will this live FOREVER?" |
| Reorganization | Frequent, expected | Rare, stable |
| Best for | Dynamic workloads | Long-term archive |

## Evolution (2022-2026)

Core PARA/CODE/Progressive Summarization unchanged since 2022 book. Evolution is in application context (now AI-augmented) and community infrastructure. Forte is pivoting to "AI-first" curriculum — democratize AI the way BASB democratized PKM.

## Patterns for Personal Space

| Pattern | Application | Priority |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| PARA's actionability principle | Organize personal docs by "how active is this?" not by topic | High |
| CODE's express step | Every input must produce output — no passive consumption | High |
| Progressive summarization | Layer notes over time instead of one-shot processing | Medium |
| Unified personal/professional | One system, not two silos | High |
| Archives as historical record | Keep everything, just deprioritize | Low |

## What NOT to Steal

- PARA as-is (too focused on documents, not life design)
- The "capture everything" mindset (leads to graveyard)
- Notion/Evernote-centric implementation (we're file-system-based)
