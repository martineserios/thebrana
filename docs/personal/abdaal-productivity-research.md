## Feel-Good Productivity Framework

Inverts traditional productivity: **energy management over time management**. Productivity flows from feeling good, not from discipline.

### Three Energizers
- **Play** — inject fun and lightness into work; sincerity + laughter
- **Power** — personal empowerment, sense of control
- **People** — surround yourself with cheerleaders, not vampires

### Three Phases
1. **Energize** — build and maintain energy
2. **Unblock** — address procrastination root causes
3. **Sustain** — create lasting systems

Grounded in neurobiology: endorphins, dopamine, oxytocin, serotonin from feeling good create energy for output.

## Life Wheel (9 Dimensions)

1. Body (physical health)
2. Mind (mental/emotional balance)
3. Soul (spiritual well-being)
4. Mission (career fulfillment)
5. Money (financial well-being)
6. Growth (personal/professional development)
7. Relationships
8. Impact
9. Fun

Simple dimensional assessment — rate each, identify imbalances, focus attention.

## Journaling Practice

- **Tool:** Day One app (digital, always accessible)
- **Multiple journals:** Main (brain dumps), Nice Comments (saves positive feedback), Memorabilia
- **Philosophy:** Quick (one sentence enough), Easy (prompts reduce friction), Enjoyable (music, coffee)
- **"No Planning" morning rule:** First 4 hours = doing only, no planning
- 8-10 recurring prompts covering clarity, purpose, decision-making

## Content Creation System

- **Notion:** backbone of all operations
- **Todoist:** daily tasks (deliberately NOT Notion databases)
- **Pipeline:** Shoot → Edit (Final Cut) → Transcribe (Subly) → Caption → Redistribute
- **AI use:** ChatGPT for thinking/planning/research (not content generation)
- **Core belief:** Human authenticity will become MORE valuable as AI commoditizes generic content

## MILES Framework (Unfair Advantages)

From Ash Ali & Hasan Kubba, popularized by Abdaal:
- **M**oney — capital you have or can access
- **I**ntelligence/Insight — book smarts, EQ, creativity
- **L**ocation/Luck — right place, right time
- **E**ducation/Expertise — specialized knowledge
- **S**tatus — social standing, credibility

Unfair advantages compound and multiply, not just add.

## Strengths

- Energy-over-efficiency is backed by real neurobiology and solves burnout
- Life wheel is simple, accessible, actionable
- Journaling practice is low-friction, high-impact
- Tool minimalism — "boring tools that work"
- Weekly reflection habit

## Weaknesses

- **Survivorship bias** — assumes baseline unfair advantages (credentials, capital, platform)
- **Scale assumptions** — strategies for 6M audience don't map to smaller operations
- **Optimization paradox** — his own regimented schedule sometimes slows creative output
- **Neurodivergence gap** — systems assume neurotypical focus
- **Influencer-optimized** — some advice is aspirational, not universally practical

## Patterns for Personal Space

| Pattern | Application | Priority |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Energy management > time management | Design around energy, not hours | High |
| Life wheel dimensional check | Periodic balance audit across 7-9 areas | High |
| Journaling with prompts | Structured daily reflection | Medium |
| "No planning" morning rule | Protect deep work from admin creep | Medium |
| Tool minimalism | Boring tools that work > shiny new apps | High |

## What NOT to Steal

- Content creation pipeline (not our use case)
- MILES framework (useful for reflection, not for system design)
- High-production workflows (solo operator, not content team)
